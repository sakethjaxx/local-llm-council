# Decision: Should We Pivot?

We are a 6-person startup, 18 months in, $800K raised (seed). Advise on the pivot decision.

## Current Product

B2B SaaS: automated invoice reconciliation for mid-market accounting teams.
MRR: $4,200 (14 customers, avg $300/mo)
Churn: 3 customers lost last quarter
Sales cycle: 45–90 days
NPS: 32

## Proposed Pivot

Consumer app: AI-powered personal expense tracker with receipt scanning.
Market: 80M gig workers in the US underserved by existing apps.
Estimated dev time to MVP: 3 months.
Revenue model: $9/mo subscription.

## Financials

Runway: 7 months at current burn ($38K/mo)
Team: 2 engineers, 1 designer, 1 sales, 1 ops, 1 founder-CEO

## What We've Heard From Customers

- "Love the reconciliation, hate the onboarding" (3 of 14 customers)
- Lost 2 customers to a competitor with better QuickBooks integration
- 1 enterprise prospect asked for a $50K custom deal; we said no

## The Real Question

Should we double down on B2B reconciliation, take the $50K custom deal, or pivot to consumer?
